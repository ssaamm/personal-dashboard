Commute Time

I track my commute time using a DO Button that logs the current time and my location to a spreadsheet. This script processes that spreadsheet and spits out data that's easier to graph with MetricsGraphics.js.

Mileage

I record my odometer's reading on a semi-regular basis in a Google Sheet. Combining that with my spending data, I know that I currently average $1.42/mi. I expect this number to come down as the costs of depreciation, tax/registration, and insurance amortize.
